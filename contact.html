Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about contacting us

What are your working hours?

+

Our office is open Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturdays from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

How long does it take to get a response?

+

We typically respond to emails within 24 hours on working days. For phone calls and WhatsApp messages, we respond as soon as possible during working hours.

Can I visit your office?

+

Yes, we welcome visitors by appointment. Please contact us in advance to schedule a visit so we can ensure someone is available to meet with you.

Do you have regional offices?

+

Yes, we have our main office in Great Accra and a regional office in Tamale, Northern Region. Contact information for both offices is provided above.

What's the best way to reach you for urgent matters?

+

For urgent matters, please call our primary number (+233 24 967 9160) or use WhatsApp for immediate attention during working hours.
